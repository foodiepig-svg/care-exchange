## Team Values

### 1. Participant First
Every product decision starts with: "Does this give participants more control and reduce their coordination burden?"

### 2. NDIS-Native
We build for the NDIS ecosystem specifically. Not adapted from another market — purpose-built for how Australian disability care actually works.

### 3. Radical Simplicity
Complex needs require simple tools. If it takes more than 5 minutes to learn, we've failed. Progress notes should be written in minutes, not hours.

### 4. Compliance by Design
NDIS Commission requirements aren't a checkbox — they're baked into the data model. Audit trails, consent records, and report generation are core features, not add-ons.

### 5. Over Communicate
Our customers are managing complex lives. We communicate clearly, proactively, and frequently — especially when things go wrong.

## Hiring Philosophy

We're building a team that can operate with high autonomy and deep domain expertise:

- **Hire for domain knowledge first.** We want people who have lived the NDIS pain, not just studied it.
- **Small team, big impact.** Every person directly touches customers and product.
- **Remote-first.** The best NDIS expertise isn't in one city.
- **Transparent compensation.** Everyone knows what everyone earns.

## Culture

### How We Work
- **Weekly strategy sessions** (founder trio) + monthly full-team
- **Async-first** — most coordination over Slack and Loom
- **Quarterly in-person** — one team offsite per quarter to align
- **Customer immersion** — every team member does 2 provider/participant shadow sessions per month

### Work-Life Integration
NDIS services run 7 days a week. We model healthy boundaries — no expecting participants or coordinators to check apps outside business hours.
